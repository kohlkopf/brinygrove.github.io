Briny Grove Farmstead is a developing 10-acre orchard marrying coastal influence with regenerative agriculture. We integrate biology, mycology, and shell-based soil care to cultivate European hazelnuts, inoculated truffle hosts, and a terroir shaped by the sea.

What happens at Briny Grove

A resilient orchard designed for long-term production, learning, and memorable gatherings.

Primary production

European hazelnuts and carefully inoculated truffle hosts grown for future commercial harvests.

Regenerative soil management

Biology-forward orchard care, mycology insight, and annual oyster shell liming to build mineral-rich, living soils.

Agrotourism & hospitality

Curated event space for weddings, farm dinners, and seasonal gatherings set amid the orchard rows.

On-farm education

Workshops on sustainable orchard design, permaculture principles, and circular resource loops.

Hospitality with a coastal terroir

From intimate farm dinners to full-scale celebrations, the farmstead showcases Pacific Northwest identity and a sea-to-soil story.

Curated gatherings

Immersive experiences

Guided walks, tastings, and educational moments that connect guests to the land, fungi, and coastal inputs.
